Keto Chocolate cake


"Chocolate, pure and simple. With a little help from eggs and butter, this flourless chocolate cake never disappoints. If you can separate eggs, you can make this low carb crowd pleaser."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients:

1 tbsp butter or coconut oil, for greasing the pan

260 g dark chocolate with a minimum of 80% cocoa solids

140g butter

5 eggs

1 pinch of salt

1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

1

 Preheat the oven to 320°F (160°C).

2

 Break the chocolate into pieces and dice the butter.

 Break the chocolate into pieces and dice the butter.

4

 Separate the eggs and put the yolks and whites in separate bowls.
